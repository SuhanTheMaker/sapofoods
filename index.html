A Frictionless Workflow

Optimized for speed and accuracy during peak service hours.

Step 1

Scan & Browse

Customers scan a QR code placed on their table. The menu opens instantly on their phone with a clean interface and automatic table detection.

Step 2

Place Order

Customers browse items, add them to cart, and place orders directly from their phone—no waiting required.

Step 3

Waiter Confirmation

Orders are first received by the waiter. They review and confirm the order before sending it to the kitchen, ensuring accuracy and control.

Step 4

Kitchen & Table System

Once confirmed, orders are sent to the kitchen. Tables are tracked in real time, and each QR is linked to a specific table for seamless operations.

Step 5

Live Tracking Experience

Customers can see live updates on their order—whether it's being prepared, ready, or served—creating transparency and better experience.

Step 6

Owner Dashboard

Restaurant owners get full control through a powerful dashboard—track revenue, monitor orders, analyze performance, and manage operations in real time.

Step 7

Staff & Access Control

Control who can access what. Assign roles to staff, manage permissions, and ensure only authorized personnel can update orders or system settings.
